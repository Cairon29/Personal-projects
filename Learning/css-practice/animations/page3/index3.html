Welcome to the World of Koenigsegg

Experience the pinnacle of automotive engineering and design

Koenigsegg Regera

The Koenigsegg Regera is a cutting-edge hybrid supercar that combines a twin-turbocharged 5.0-liter V-8 engine with three electric motors to produce a staggering 1500 horsepower. This model features Koenigsegg Direct Drive technology, which eliminates the need for a traditional transmission, and boasts a world record for accelerating from zero to 249 mph and back to zero in just 31.49 seconds:refs[2-4].

Koenigsegg Agera RS

The Koenigsegg Agera RS is renowned for its speed and performance, previously holding the title of the world's fastest production car. With a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine producing 1160 horsepower, the Agera RS can achieve astonishing speeds and has set multiple speed records:refs[4-5].

Koenigsegg Jesko

The Koenigsegg Jesko is designed to push the boundaries of performance and innovation. Equipped with a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that delivers up to 1600 horsepower, the Jesko features advanced aerodynamics and a revolutionary transmission system, making it one of the most powerful and technologically advanced hypercars in the world:refs[6-1].

Koenigsegg CCXR

The Koenigsegg CCXR is a flex-fuel sports car that can run on both standard gasoline and E85 biofuel. This model is part of Koenigsegg's commitment to green technology and sustainable performance. The CCXR features a modified version of the 4.7-liter V8 engine found in the CCX, producing up to 1018 horsepower when running on E85:refs[8-1].

Koenigsegg CC8S

The Koenigsegg CC8S was the first production car from Koenigsegg, introduced in 2002. With a 4.7-liter V8 engine producing 655 horsepower, the CC8S set the stage for Koenigsegg's future models by showcasing the company's innovative engineering and design capabilities:refs[10-5].

Koenigsegg One:1

The Koenigsegg One:1 is named for its power-to-weight ratio of one horsepower per kilogram. This limited-edition hypercar features a 5.0-liter twin-turbocharged V8 engine that produces 1341 horsepower, making it one of the most powerful and exclusive cars ever produced:refs[12-9].
